FAQ

Is this legal?

Yes. Section 125 Cafeteria Plans are explicitly authorized by the Internal Revenue Code (1978). This is codified federal tax law.

Does this replace my health insurance?

No. This is a supplemental "second layer" of benefits. Your existing health, dental, and vision plans remain completely unchanged.

How is there a payroll credit?

The credit comes from FICA tax savings (7.65%) on the pre-tax funds. You save money that you would have otherwise paid to the IRS in payroll taxes.
